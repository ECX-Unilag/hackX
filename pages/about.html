What is HackX?

The ﬁrst edition of ECX Hackathon themed HackX “Building Solutions for the Future” focuses on building innovative solutions to the following sectors: Health, Real Estate, Logistics, Education. The competition enables programmers to share skills in teams solving a real engineering challenge.

Who is eligible?

The challenge is open to Nigerian undergraduates in selected colleges. There would be round(s) of shortlisting after the open application and review of qualiﬁcation by special panelists.

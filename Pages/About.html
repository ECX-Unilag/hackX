What Is HackX?

The first edition of ECX Hackathon themed HackX "Building Solutions For The Future" focuses on building innovative solutions to the following sectors: Health, Real Estate, Logistics, Education. The competition enables programmers to share skills in teams solving a real Engineering challenge.

Who Is Eligible?

The challenge is open to Nigerian undergraduates in selected colleges. There would be round(s) of shortlisting after the open application and review of qualification by special panelists.

CRITERIA FOR SELECTION

  • Selection would be aimed at giving equal gender oppurtunities
  • Participants must have active WhatsApp contact, email address, Github account for ease of communication
  • Each team would have a range of 4 - 5 individuals.
  • The Hackathon is only opened to undergraduates of qualified colleges as stated
  • The admin would select based on the merit discretion of qualified candidates
  • A college can have more thsn one person for representation
